# Privacy Policy

**Dialed App**
Last updated: April 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Dialed ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Dialed mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly:
- **Account information:** display name, email address, username, and password
- **Profile information:** profile photo, bio, and location (optional)
- **Content:** habits you create, posts you share, comments, and direct messages
- **Phone number** (optional, if provided in settings)

### Information collected automatically:
- **Usage analytics:** events such as habits created, habits logged, features used, screens visited, and session activity
- **Device information:** device type and operating system version
- **App version and platform** (iOS/Android)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Display your profile and content to other users (based on your privacy settings)
- Send notifications about activity related to your account
- Improve, maintain, and develop new features for the App
- Analyze usage patterns to understand how the App is used
- Respond to support requests

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information (name, email, messages, or private content) to third parties.

We may share information in the following ways:

- **With other users:** Content you post publicly is visible to other Dialed users. You control visibility in your settings.
- **Anonymized and aggregated analytics:** We may share or license anonymized, aggregate usage data (e.g., overall feature usage trends, retention statistics) with analytics partners or business partners. This data cannot be used to identify you individually.
- **Service providers:** We use Railway for infrastructure hosting. Your data is stored securely on their platform.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
- **Business transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway. We use industry-standard practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) to protect your information. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information through your profile settings
- **Delete** your account and associated data through the App settings (Settings → Delete account) or by contacting us
- **Opt out** of push notifications through your device settings

To request data access or deletion, contact us at: **support@dialed.app**

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal information, posts, habits, and messages are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Anonymized, aggregate analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be used to identify you.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
