## 2. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly:
- **Account information:** name, email address, username, and password
- **Profile information:** profile photo, bio, location (optional)
- **Content:** habits you create, posts you share, comments, and messages you send to other users
- **Phone number** (optional, if provided in settings)

### Information collected automatically:
- **Usage data:** how you interact with the App (features used, screens visited)
- **Device information:** device type, operating system version

### Information we do NOT collect:
- We do not collect precise GPS location
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising

We use your information to:
