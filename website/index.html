How it works

Simple enough
to stick to.

01
Create your habits

Set up any habit — daily, weekly, or monthly. Pick a color, set an optional reminder, and choose who can see when you miss a day.

02
Log and build streaks

One tap to log. Watch your streak grow and your calendar fill in. Hit milestones at Day 7, 30, 100 — the app celebrates them with you.

03
Share with your circle

Post your logs to a social feed. Follow friends, see their habits, and let accountability do the work motivation can't.
